welcome To CCI Matasia

Welcome to our church! We are a diverse and vibrant community of believers with a common purpose: to proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ and serve His Kingdom with love and grace. Our mission is to be a place where people can come to encounter God, to grow in their faith, and to serve others in the name of Jesus. .
We believe that faith is more than just attending church on Sunday. We are committed to helping each other grow in our faith through prayer, Bible study, and acts of service. We also strive to be a community where everyone feels welcome and accepted. We invite you to explore our website and join us for worship. We look forward to meeting you and sharing the love of Jesus Christ!
