Kids

Age 1-12

The kids ministry at our church is a vital part of our faith community. We strive to create a safe, nurturing, and fun environment for our little ones to learn the word of God and grow in their faith. We want to give these children the best start possible as they explore their faith and find their place in the church family.

Our kids ministry offers a variety of activities and programs that are tailored to the needs of each individual child. We strive to provide a variety of activities that will encourage children to explore their faith and develop a relationship with God. Our Sunday school classes provide an opportunity for kids to learn about the Bible and to develop a deeper understanding of their faith. We also offer weekly activities such as crafts, music, and games that are designed to be fun and engaging.

It is our hope that our kids ministry will be a place where children can feel welcomed, valued, and loved. We want our kids to know that they are part of a larger family, and that the church is a place where they can feel safe and secure. We strive to provide an environment where our children can ask questions, explore their faith, and be encouraged in their spiritual growth.
