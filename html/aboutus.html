ABOUT CCI MATASIA

Who are we

CCI MATASIA is community that redefines the idea of “church” for the 21st century: a gathering of real people in a modern worship experience where Christ is encountered in culturally relevant ways, and where the radical expression of faith spurs our people to spread the love of Christ to all of eastern Iowa and beyond.

Leadership

Rev Benjamin Mbithi

Lead-Pastor

As the Lead Pastor of our church, I am excited to welcome you to our faith community. My goal is to help us grow in our relationship with God, and to share the love of Jesus with our neighbors. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere that supports the spiritual growth of all who come to our church. We offer a variety of ministries and activities that cater to all ages and levels of faith. Our goal is to help everyone find an area of ministry that best fits their gifts, talents, and needs.

We also strive to reach out to our community with the love of Jesus. We are proud to be a part of the local neighborhood and to be involved in local outreach efforts. I am committed to building relationships and partnerships with other churches, organizations, and individuals to share the good news of Jesus.I invite you to join us as we grow in faith and share the love of Jesus with others. I look forward to getting to know you and your family as we journey together in faith.

Our Mission

The mission of our church is to proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ and to nurture believers in their faith, so that together we may grow in our relationship with God and serve others with love and grace.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a vibrant, diverse, and welcoming community of faith where all are accepted, encouraged, and supported in their spiritual journeys. We strive to be a beacon of hope and a place of refuge for all people in our community and beyond.

What We Believe

1.

God is Good

Our company believe in one timeless God who is the Designer of all things. He exists in 3 Persons: God the Papa, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit. He is entirely caring and totally divine.

2.

Nothing Is Impossible

We are dedicated to inspiring and uplifting stories about faith, hope, and the power of prayer. This could be a place where visitors can read about answered prayers, find encouragement during difficult times, or even submit their own stories of God's guidance and provision. This could be a great way to remind people that nothing is impossible when we put our trust in the Lord.

3.

We are Significant

We are passionate about offering the worldwide neighborhood as demonstrating God's love to others. Whether personally or online, people are in our church and all over the world.
